You, me, the Secret Society of Hot Chicks...

I'd say, in a perfect world, you'd get to go to college with all of your buds that you ran with in High School. This comic is about that. A tight group of friends led by Jack, then Animal, Jonesy, Merv and their pets: Shamrock the dog, and Scooter, the two legged cat.

I write the comic to get all the ideas out I have about all the stuff I experienced in college, all the stuff I wish I did, and all of the people I met and conspiratory groups I was certain existed.

Check out the comic, you can read as far back as my (re)start back in 2004, all the way to 2006; catch up on the gang, the Secret Society of Hot Chicks, and everyone in between.

Enjoy,

Nick
