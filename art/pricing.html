Pricing

On each piece that is still available for sale, I have a suggested price.

This price, however, isn't set in stone.

If you're interested in multiple paintings I'm willing to haggle, make deal packages, and the like.

Ultimately, for all of the hard work and creativity I put into my work, to cover cost of materials and the all of the paint fumes I had to inhale in order to make the piece, the listed compensation would be nice.

I'm alway open for best offers, the painting, after all, is meant to be hung on your wall, and not sit around my studio collecting dust.

~Nick
