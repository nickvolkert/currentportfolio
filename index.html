Experienced UX/UI/Visual Designer & Illustrator!

My name is Nick Volkert, I currently reside in Greater Chicago, IL.

I'm currently a UX/UI and Visual Designer at Digital Initiatives, LLC, a full-service startup incubator in Hillside, IL.

You can see my branding, visual design & UX/UI work at Prudentpet.com, MoveMatcher.com, Claimpix.com and BlueStarRelo.com.

I also maintain the website designs of Prescient.com, Bluestarautomovers.com, and MTHIndustries.com

Samples are available upon request!
